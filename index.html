ORIGIN
In West Africa, newborns are given unique names accoridng to their day of the week when they are born. It symbolizes solidarily of the soceity and ancestral affiliation. The days have meanings about the soul and character of the person. Examples of significant figures who had these names are Kwame Nkrumah and Kofi Annan.
